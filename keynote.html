meet our keynote speaker

Creator of #RejectionHotline & 50+ viral products

LinkedIn Profile

Jeff Goldblatt is an Atlanta-based Entrepreneur with 20 years of experience in B2C Startups of many shapes and sizes (including: VC-funded, Angel-funded, and Bootstrapped… successes, failures, and that subjective gray area between… as a Founder, CoFounder, CEO, Creative Director, Consultant…) and, in recent years, as Advisor/Mentor/Coach to 100+ young founders. Originally from Massachusetts, he has an MBA from Emory University and a PhD from The Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry.

As creator of the original "Rejection Hotline" (a satirical fake phone number service that went super-viral, led to hundreds of other “Humor Hotlines” and evolved into a 7-figure viral content company engaging millions of fans every month), he became a nationally-recognized expert on Viral Products, Viral Content, Viral Marketing, and "VIRAL THINKING" (the title of the most popular startup workshop he has taught over the years).

He feels awkward writing about himself in the 3rd Person, so he will now switch to 1st Person (and so will I)…

I struggle writing bios like this because it’s easy/expected to just cherry-pick your most impressive-sounding accomplishments, awards/recognitions, maybe drop some big names and numbers… But I think that does a disservice to FUTURE Founders who rarely get full stories; so…

#BeReal – Like MOST entrepreneurs, I put “CEO” on my first business card. Also like most entrepreneurs, I once believed in The Tooth Fairy. In BOTH cases, it took YEARS to realize I WAS WRONG:

Despite the publicly-visible success on the product/content/fanbase side of my first business, I was severely OVERWHELMED “wearing all the hats” as an inexperienced solo founder. I had MINIMAL REVENUE and I was LOSING MONEY each month. I began struggling with my MENTAL HEALTH, experienced my first PANIC ATTACK, and considered QUITTING many times.

Finally, I made a (long-overdue) decision: I FIRED MYSELF AS CEO OF MY OWN COMPANY and brought in a partner with more experience and skills complementary to mine… [Within 3 months, we raised $1 MILLION in Angel-Funding, and within 9 months, we were a PROFITABLE 7-figure company – (after YEARS of losing money on my own!) – #BestDecisionEver]

My #IamNOTaCEO epiphany was undoubtedly the catalyst for my first startup success; and it began my fascination (obsession?!) with #SelfAwareness (or lack thereof!) and the MANY related problems/pitfalls that MANY young entrepreneurs are OFTEN blind to (until YEARS LATER!), often evoking the all-too-common retrospective refrain: “I Wish I Knew THEN, What I Know NOW!”

Fast-Forward (more years than I care to admit) – I don’t mean to be a “Negative Nancy” or “Debbie Downer” (I prefer “Realistic Rick”), but after analyzing 500 FAILED FOUNDERS (via 500Failures.com & ReThinkingStartups.com), it is a dumbfounding reality that we see startups/founders failing today, not only at the same rates, but for many/most of the same collective reasons(!) as startups/founders 5 years ago and 10 years ago and…

We have some Innovative Improvement Initiatives and new “Startup *S.H.I.T. to Share” *[Strategies, Hypotheses, Insights, Theories] intended to address #StatusQuoBias (in Startup Communities and Early Entrepreneur Education) and to be a game-changer in PRE-startup preparation for FUTURE FOUNDERS and their journeys ahead – and I’ll be talking about it all publicly for the very first time at #HackATL 2022!
