meet our website developers

The Emory Entrepreneurship & Venture Management (EEVM) Tech Division manitains and develops EEVM’s technological needs, as well as assisting the Emory community by creating technological solutions for on-campus groups such as research labs and startups. Working very closely with the HackATL Co-Directors, our largest project is designing and developing HackATL's website to fit the annual theme. As a team, we focus on learning, expanding, and strengthening real-world skills related to coding, development, UI/UX design, website management, and much more. If you are interested in joining a group of passionate, driven, and creative developers, look no further than the Tech Division!
